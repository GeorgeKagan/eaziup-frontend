How it works

EaZiUp is a platform that connects entrepreneurs to students seeking interesting projects with added value, as opposed to just getting it done and throwing it away. Entrepreneurs' benefit from the exchange is that they pay far less than they would if they had chosen a freelancer.

From an entrepreneur's perspective
Submitting a project

Have a project in mind, but limited resources? Sign up and begin by filling the new project form.

Provide as much details as possible, as it's crucial to minimize miscommunication.

Selecting a team

Choose from teams of students that found your project interesting and would like work on it.

You can see exactly who are the candidates and how qualified they are.

Receiving the project

The project is delivered to you incrementally, as the milestones you defined complete.

On each milestone's completion, you release a part of the funds to pay for the students' work.

From a student's perspective
Browsing projects

Browse verified projects and choose the one that interests you the most.

Applying to a project

Create a team that consists of your co-students and apply as one.

Starting to work

As a project owner selects your team, you can begin to work!
